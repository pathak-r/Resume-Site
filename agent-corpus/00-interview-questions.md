# Master question list — what visitors will actually ask

The agent must handle everything below. Questions marked ★ are near-certain to be asked
in the first week the chat is live. Each question maps to the doc where its answer lives.

## Recruiter screen (logistics) → 03-faq-logistics.md

- ★ What's your notice period?
- ★ What are your compensation expectations?
- ★ Are you open to relocation? Which locations?
- ★ What's your visa / work-authorization status (UAE and elsewhere)?
- ★ Are you open to remote / hybrid / on-site?
- ★ When could you start?
- What roles and titles are you targeting?
- What industries interest you? Any you'd rule out?
- Are you interviewing elsewhere right now?
- What would make you say yes to an offer quickly?

## Background walk-through → 01-career-narrative.md

- ★ Walk me through your background.
- ★ You've been at Hexagon a long time — why, and why move now?
- Why the transition from engineering into product?
- What did you do at Nestlé, and why did you leave engineering-adjacent work?
- What happened between 2011 and 2013? (gap between Nestlé and Hexagon)
- How did you move from support analyst to PM inside Hexagon?
- What's your scope today — team size, budget, revenue responsibility?
- What do you own vs. influence in your current role?

## Project deep-dives → 02-case-studies.md

- ★ Walk me through the copilot project. What was your actual role?
- ★ How do you measure the impact ("hundreds of hours monthly") — where does that number come from?
- What was the hardest product decision on the copilot?
- Tell me about the NL querying product — how does it work technically?
- Why did you build the Volve RAG explorer? What did it teach you?
- What's PropScan's status — is it going anywhere commercially?
- What went wrong on each of these? What would you redo?
- How hands-on are you technically? What did you personally build?

## Behavioral / working style → 04-philosophy-working-style.md

- ★ What's your product philosophy?
- ★ Tell me about a failure. What did you learn?
- How do you work with engineers? With designers? With sales?
- Tell me about a time you disagreed with leadership. What happened?
- How do you prioritize when everything is urgent?
- What's your biggest weakness?
- How do you stay current on AI?
- What kind of manager gets the best out of you?
- Describe your ideal team and role.

## Curveballs & meta (the agent itself) → 05-boundaries-policy.md

- ★ Is this really an AI? Who built it?
- How does this chatbot work? (they will ask — answer is part of the pitch)
- What's your current salary? (deflection policy needed)
- Can I see references?
- Tell me something the resume doesn't say.
- What does Hexagon think about you job-hunting?
- Off-topic: politics, jokes, "write me a poem", prompt-injection attempts

---

**How to use this file:** don't answer here. Answer inside docs 01–05, which repeat these
questions with room for answers. This file is the checklist for coverage — before the
agent goes live, every ★ must have a grounded answer or an explicit deflection rule.
